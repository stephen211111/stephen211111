## Background

My path into energy storage started with materials and manufacturing — scan strategies for selective laser melting, a filed patent on metal additive manufacturing, and a series of self-directed hardware projects built as a teenager. That hands-on foundation shaped how I approach engineering problems at Terminus: build it, measure it, iterate.

Terminus Energy is focused on replacing lithium-ion BESS in applications where batteries are structurally disadvantaged — EV fast charging, depot charging, and daily energy trading. High cycle counts erode battery economics and longevity fast. Flywheels don't degrade.

National-level competitive swimmer (Poland). Based in Copenhagen.
